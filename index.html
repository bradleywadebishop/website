About

Wade Bishop is a Professor in the School of Information Sciences at the University of Tennessee-Knoxville. At the College of Communication and Information, he serves as the Associate Dean for Faculty Development. He is also the School of Information Sciences Director of Graduate Studies and the Research Data Management Certificate Coordinator. In addition, he serves as a Research Security Ambassador for the Office of Research, Innovation, and Economic Development. His research focus is on Research Data Management, Data Discovery, Geographic Information Science, as well as the study of data occupations, education, and training. He has other research expertise that includes physical access for users to U.S. public libraries (using Geographic information Systems (GIS)) and the evaluation of many other services and resources in academic and public libraries. Bishop has an M.L.I.S. from the University of South Florida School of Information and a Ph.D. from Florida State University's School of Information. He serves as Associate Editor for Telematics and Informatics and on several other editorial boards.
